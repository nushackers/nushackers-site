Thank you all for coming down to Hack&Roll 2026! 🎉 

It was an absolute blast for us to organise Singapore's largest student-run hackathon once again! When we plan this event every January, our goal is always to celebrate true hacker culture and create an environment where you can have the freedom to simply build for fun.

This year, we introduced something special to the Hack&Roll pacakge, our first ever custom PCB badge! Seeing you personalize, color, and hack these hardware badges throughout the weekend perfectly captured the maker spirit that we love so much.

A massive shoutout to our incredible sponsors and partners! Thanks to their generous support, we were able to distribute over **8,550** pieces of swag to the community.

Here is a look at the madness of H&R 2026, by the numbers 📈

* We hosted an incredible **809** participants across **297** teams! 
* **138** judges came down to interact with your projects
* We gave away **8,550** pieces of swag
* To keep the energy up for **24** hours of fun & hacking, we demolished **130** pizzas and **3,264** drinks consumed
* For the side quests, there were **39** ducks found and **5** Typeracer games played, featuring a crazy record of **156** WPM!
* All of this was made possible by our dedicated crew of **59** organisers and **1** happy duck
